Online Experiment Notes

Before doing the experiment

  • Please turn off any music or videos now.
  • Please mute or turn off your phone and put it out of your reach.
  • Please make sure you won't be distracted during the experiment (e.g., your pets or your flatmates).

During the experiment

Do not leave this tab (your browser window) during the experiment and do not reload this tab! If you do switch tabs/windows during the experiment (to check your mail or visit another website, for example), the experiment will end automatically and you will not receive credit for your participation. Repeated participation or resumption of the experiment is impossible.

After finishing the experiment

After finishing the experiment, you will automatically be redirected to the Prolific website. Closing the browser window prematurely will make the allocation of your participation credit impossible.

I agree that I will not receive credit if I fail to follow the rules above.
